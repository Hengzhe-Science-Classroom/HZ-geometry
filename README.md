# Geometry · Interactive

**Euclidean geometry, trigonometry, conic sections**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 2 · High School

## What You'll Learn

- Euclidean geometry fundamentals
- Triangle congruence and similarity
- Circles and their properties
- Trigonometric functions
- Conic sections: parabolas, ellipses, hyperbolas

## References

- 人教版《普通高中教科书 · 数学》必修第二册
- Alexander & Koeberlein, *Elementary Geometry for College Students* (7th ed.)
- Moise & Downs, *Geometry*

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
